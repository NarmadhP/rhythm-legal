Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Narmadh P ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Rhythm mobile application ("the App" or "the Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Medication Data

Local Storage Only

All health and medication data you enter into the Rhythm app is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Medication names and dosages
  • Medication schedules and reminders
  • Medication history and logs
  • Any other health-related information you input

We do not collect, transmit, or store this sensitive health information on our servers or share it with third parties. This data remains under your control and is accessible only through your device.

1.2 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect information through Firebase Authentication, which may include:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed)
  • Account creation date

This information is necessary to provide you with account functionality and secure access to your account across devices.

1.3 Usage Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the App. This information may include:

  • App usage patterns and frequency
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App version information
  • Crash reports and error logs

Important: Google Analytics does not collect your personal health data or medication information. All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

1.4 Subscription and Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases or subscribe to premium features, RevenueCat processes your purchase information. This may include:

  • Purchase history
  • Subscription status
  • Transaction identifiers

We do not have access to your payment card information, which is handled securely by the app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your account and provide secure access
  • Process your subscription and in-app purchases
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send you important updates about the Service (if you opt-in)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process information:

3.1 Firebase Authentication

Firebase Auth is used for user authentication. Google's Firebase Privacy Policy applies to this service. You can review it at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 RevenueCat

RevenueCat manages subscriptions and in-app purchases. Their Privacy Policy can be found at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.3 Google Analytics

Google Analytics helps us understand how users interact with the App. Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by disabling it in your device settings or by using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local Data: Your health and medication data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.
  • Account Data: Account information is secured through Firebase Authentication's industry-standard security measures.
  • Transmission: All data transmitted between the App and our servers (for account and analytics purposes) is encrypted using SSL/TLS.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Health and Medication Data: This data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. We do not retain copies of this data.

Account Information: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may delete your account at any time.

Analytics Data: Google Analytics retains anonymized usage data according to Google's data retention policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Control

  • Health Data: You can access, modify, or delete your health and medication data directly within the App at any time.
  • Account Data: You can update your account information through the App settings or by contacting us.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your account information from our systems.

6.2 Data Portability

Since your health data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can export or backup this data using your device's backup features.

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking through your device settings
  • You can disable push notifications through your device settings
  • You can cancel subscriptions through your app store account settings

6.4 California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. Note that we do not sell your personal information.

6.5 European Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal information
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to object
  • The right to restriction of processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to these facilities.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law. Given that health data is stored locally on your device, the risk of such breaches affecting your health information is minimized.
